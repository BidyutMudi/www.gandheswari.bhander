About Gandheswari Bhander

Gandheswari Bhander is a family-owned grocery store located in the heart of Anara, Purulia. We specialize in providing fresh vegetables, quality grains, dairy products, spices, and household essentials at affordable prices. Since our inception, we have been committed to serving the local community with honesty and trust. Our mission is to ensure every household has access to quality food and daily necessities without compromise.
